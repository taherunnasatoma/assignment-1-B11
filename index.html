Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating and implementing new ideas, methods, or technologies to solve problems or improve existing systems. It drives progress by fostering creativity and delivering value across various industries.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own. It focuses on balancing environmental, social, and economic considerations to ensure long-term well-being for people and the planet.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the basic facilities and systems serving a country, city, or area, such as transportation, power plants, and school

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of gaining the ability to make choices, have influence, and control over one's life. It can also refer to the principles that allow individuals and groups to act in ways that ensure their well-being
